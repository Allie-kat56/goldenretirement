Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Gold IRA? A self-directed retirement account that holds physical gold.
  • Is gold safe for seniors? Yes, gold can hedge against inflation and economic uncertainty.
  • How do I open one? Through custodians such as Augusta, Birch, or Goldco.
  • Minimum investment? Typically $10,000–$50,000 depending on provider.
  • Can I transfer a 401(k)? Yes, rollovers are tax-advantaged when handled properly.
  • Are Gold IRAs insured? Reputable custodians provide insurance coverage for stored metals.
