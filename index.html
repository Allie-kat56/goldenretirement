Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Gold IRA? → A self-directed retirement account that holds physical gold instead of paper assets.
  • Is gold a safe investment for seniors? → Gold is often considered a hedge against inflation and market downturns.
  • How do I open a Gold IRA? → You can open one through a custodian like Augusta, Birch, or Goldco.
  • What’s the minimum investment for a Gold IRA? → Typically $10,000–$50,000 depending on the provider.
  • Can I transfer my 401(k) into a Gold IRA? → Yes, rollovers are common and tax-advantaged when done correctly.
  • Are Gold IRAs insured? → Reputable custodians offer insurance for stored metals to protect your assets.
